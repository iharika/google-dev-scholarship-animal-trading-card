Maltese

Sporting a thick coat of hair instead of fur, these little dogs don’t shed. Their white tufts are hypoallergenic, making them great for families with allergies.

  • Hypoallergenic: Yes
  • Life span: 12 - 15 years
  • Origin: Mediterranean Basin
  • Temperament: Affectionate, Playful, Active
  • Height: Male: 21–25 cm, Female: 20–23 cm
  • Weight: Male: 3–4 kg, Female: 3–4 kg

A dog breed who is gentle and fearless, the Maltese greets everyone as a friend. His glamorous white coat gives him a look of haughty nobility, but looks can be deceiving. But most of all, he loves to be with his people.
